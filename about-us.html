About Us

About Company

E7. The name stands for “7 Engineers”, tagging 7 of our friends. The adrenaline rush for entrepreneurship in us had pulled this name for a café we planned in early 2011. However, like every amateur entrepreneurship story it saw the end even before coming to papers. However, after many years of aspiration, gaining knowledge on business and lessons learnt from traveling, an idea from one of the partners Er Yahyaa, became a concept. Further, with support from Er Sasi, Er Kamal and Er Sura@ Surendran the concept took shape as a adventure company. Thus name E7, from being a whatsapp group since 2011 became an official letterhead and E7 Adventures incorporated on the New year’s day of 2018. Presently, we are the Activity partners of Sterling Holidays Pvt. Ltd., at “Sterling - Kodai by the Lake” and Thomas Cook (India) Pvt. Ltd., at “Kodai by the Valley”, Kodaikanal.
